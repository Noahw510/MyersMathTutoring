Do you have a Middle or High school student looking to boost their math skills?

I am Noah Myers, a current junior math major at Liberty University with a passion for making complicated math understandable. I am eager to help middle and high school students to gain a better understanding of math and improve their skills as well as their grades. For many students, mathematics seems incredibly complicated; however, it is my belief that through gaining an intuitive understanding of the concepts taught in their classes, they can achieve great success.
Math Excellence Through Myers Math Tutoring, I provide one-on-one, personalized teaching and review for students in pre-algebra all the way to calculus. Both in-person and online tutoring are offered. * When teaching online, I make use of Zoom and its interactive whiteboard feature to facilitate concepts and to observe students as they work. This gives virtual lessons the same effectiveness as in-person.
